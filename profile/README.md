## Hi there 👋

# Amplifi

**Technology that opens doors to essential resources.**

Amplifi is a Los Angeles-based 501(c)(3) nonprofit that co-designs digital tools to simplify the complex process of connecting people to the benefits, resources, and support they need.

---

### 🚀 Our flagship product: [Benefit Navigator](https://www.amplifi.org/benefit-navigator)

Benefit Navigator is an advanced, human-centered technology platform built by and for social service providers. It helps case workers and their clients confidently:

- Quickly access benefits, tax credits, and local resources
- Proactively navigate policy changes like HR 1
- Identify Benefit Cliffs and viable workforce pathways
- Track progress with unprecedented impact data

### 🌍 We also build tools like the [LA Disaster Relief Navigator](https://www.amplifi.org/disaster-navigator)

Created in the wake of the LA wildfires, the Disaster Relief Navigator connects people to life-saving relief resources in times of crisis.

---

### 💡 Why we do it

We believe people need better ways to navigate complex systems. Too many families leave billions of dollars in untapped benefits on the table — not because help doesn't exist, but because finding it is overwhelming.

Our tools turn labyrinthine systems into clear, dignified pathways toward resilience and economic mobility.

---

### 📊 Our impact

Since 2023, the Benefit Navigator and LA Disaster Relief Navigator have helped:

- **25,000+ households** in Los Angeles and beyond
- Identify **$230+ Million** in critical resources

---

### 🧭 How we work

Our founding principles guide everything we build:

- **A commitment to building trust** — rigorously vetted, actionable information
- **Human-centered at our core** — the people who use our tools shape them
- **Listening, adapting, and evolving** — responsive to community feedback
- **Technology that helps humans thrive** — simplifying systems, never replacing connection

We also run the **Amplifi Innovation Lab (iLab)**, where we evolve technologies — including agentic AI — alongside our community partners.
